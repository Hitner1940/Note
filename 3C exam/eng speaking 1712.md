### 第一部分：萬能演講框架 (Universal Framework)

無論題目是什麼，你都可以套用這個結構。這能確保你的演講條理清晰，時間控制得當。

#### 1. 開場白 (Introduction) - 約 15 秒

考試卷上通常已經給了第一句，**一定要讀出來**，然後稍微解釋一下背景。

- **Template (模板):**
    
    > "Good morning/afternoon, everybody. I'm going to talk about [Topic Name]. This is an important topic because..." (大家早/午安。我將談論 [題目]。這是一個重要的話題，因為……)
    

#### 2. 主體內容 (Body Paragraphs) - 約 1 分 30 秒

這是核心部分。你需要把 Mind Map 上的 3-4 個分支（Branches）變成 3-4 個重點。 **關鍵技巧：使用連接詞 (Transition Words) 來告訴考官你換重點了。**

- **Point 1 (第一點):**
    
    > "First of all, let's look at [Branch 1, e.g., the reasons]. We need to..." (首先，讓我們看看 [分支1]。我們需要……) _加上 1-2 句解釋或例子。_
    
- **Point 2 (第二點):**
    
    > "Secondly, regarding [Branch 2, e.g., the methods]. I suggest that we can..." (其次，關於 [分支2]。我建議我們可以……)
    
- **Point 3 (第三點):**
    
    > "Another important aspect is [Branch 3, e.g., the problems/benefits]. For example,..." (另一個重要的方面是 [分支3]。舉例來說，……)
    
- **Own Idea (自己的想法 - 這是加分項!):**
    
    > "Besides the ideas on the mind map, I also think that..." (除了思維導圖上的想法，我還認為……)
    

#### 3. 結尾 (Conclusion) - 約 15 秒

簡單總結，並讀出試卷底部的結束語。

- **Template (模板):**
    
    > "To sum up, [Topic Name] is really beneficial/necessary. I hope everyone can support this idea." (總而言之，[題目] 是非常有益/必要的。我希望大家能支持這個想法。) "That’s the end of my presentation. Thank you."

### 第二部分：針對性詞彙與句型 (Topic-Specific Content)

這裡針對你上傳的三個題目，提供具體的可以用得上的英文。

#### Topic 1: Raising Money for the English Corner (為英語角籌款)


**核心詞彙 (Vocabulary):**

- **Renovation (翻新):** To repair and improve a building/room.
    
- **Outdated (過時的):** Old-fashioned.
    
- **Facilities (設施):** Equipment provided for a particular purpose.
    
- **Charity Sale (義賣):** Selling goods to raise money.
    
- **Generous (慷慨的):** Willing to give more (money/help).
    

**實用句型 (Sentences):**

- **關於原因 (Why):** "The current furniture is old and broken. We need money for **renovation** to make the place more attractive." (目前的家具又舊又破。我們需要資金翻新，讓這個地方更吸引人。)
    
- **關於方法 (How):** "We can organize a **charity sale** during lunch time. Students can donate their old books or toys to sell." (我們可以在午飯時間舉辦義賣。學生可以捐出舊書或玩具來賣。)
    
- **關於購買物品 (What to buy):** "We should buy some **tablets (平板電腦)** and English magazines so students can learn English in a fun way." (我們應該買些平板電腦和英文雜誌，讓學生能更有趣地學英文。)

#### Topic 2: Planning a Social Service Visit to a Kindergarten (計劃幼兒園探訪)


**核心詞彙 (Vocabulary):**

- **Interact (互動):** Communicate or be involved directly.
    
- **Patience (耐心):** The capacity to accept or tolerate delay/trouble.
    
- **Responsibility (責任感):** The state or fact of having a duty to deal with something.
    
- **Lively (活潑的):** Full of life and energy.
    
- **Atmosphere (氣氛):** The pervading tone or mood of a place.
    

**實用句型 (Sentences):**

- **關於好處 (Benefits):** "This visit helps us **develop team spirit** and learn how to take care of small kids with **patience**." (這次探訪能幫助我們培養團隊精神，並學習如何耐心地照顧小孩。)
    
- **關於活動 (Activities):** "We can organize some **group games** like 'Musical Chairs'. Also, we can hold a coloring **competition**." (我們可以組織一些團體遊戲，像大風吹。也可以舉辦填色比賽。)
    
- **關於時間 (When):** "Since students are free on weekends, **Saturday morning** is the best time for the visit." (因為學生週末有空，週六早上是探訪的最佳時間。)

#### Topic 3: Watching Films and TV to Learn English (看電影電視學英文)


**核心詞彙 (Vocabulary):**

- **Authentic (道地的/真實的):** Genuine, real (e.g., authentic accent).
    
- **Subtitles (字幕):** Captions displayed at the bottom of a screen.
    
- **Pronunciation (發音):** The way in which a word is pronounced.
    
- **Slang (俚語):** Very informal language.
    
- **Accessible (容易取得的):** Easy to get or use.
    

**實用句型 (Sentences):**

- **關於原因 (Reasons):** "Watching films is **entertaining**. It offers something for everyone, regardless of their interests." (看電影很有娛樂性。無論大家的興趣是什麼，總能找到適合的內容。)
    
- **關於方法 (How):** "Nowadays, using **live-streaming platforms** like Netflix or Disney+ is very convenient." (現在，使用像 Netflix 或 Disney+ 這樣的串流平台非常方便。)
    
- **關於問題 (Problems):** "Sometimes the **actors speak too quickly**. To solve this, we can turn on the English **subtitles** to follow along." (有時候演員說話太快了。為了解決這個問題，我們可以開啟英文字幕跟著看。)
