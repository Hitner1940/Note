## 📘 Revision Summary (English + Key Chinese Terms)

### Chapter 4 – Percentages (II) 百分數 (II)

- **Percentage change**:  
    New value = Original × (1 ± percentage change rate)  
    Change rate = (New − Original)/Original × 100%
    
- **Profit / Loss**:  
    Profit% = (Selling price − Cost)/Cost × 100%  
    Loss% = (Cost − Selling price)/Cost × 100%
    
- **Discount & Tax**:  
    Selling price = Marked price × (1 − Discount%)  
    After tax = Original × (1 + Tax rate%)
    
- **Simple interest 單利息**:  
    Interest = Principal × Rate × Time  
    Amount = Principal(1 + r·t)
    
- **Compound interest 複利息**:  
    Amount = Principal(1 + r)ⁿ  
    Compound interest = Amount − Principal
    
- **Common applications**: growth, depreciation, inflation, population change, mixing solutions

### Chapter 5 – Quadrilaterals 四邊形

- **Sum of interior angles** = (n−2)×180°  
    Quadrilateral interior angle sum = 360°
    
- **Sum of exterior angles** (convex polygon) = 360°
    
- **Parallelogram 平行四邊形**:  
    opposite sides parallel & equal, opposite angles equal, adjacent angles supplementary, diagonals bisect each other
    
- **Rectangle 矩形**: parallelogram + 4 right angles → diagonals equal
    
- **Rhombus 菱形**: parallelogram + 4 equal sides → diagonals perpendicular bisectors & angle bisectors
    
- **Square 正方形**: rectangle + rhombus (4 equal sides, 4 right angles, equal & perpendicular diagonals)
    
- **Trapezium 梯形** (UK: trapezoid): one pair of parallel sides. Isosceles trapezium: legs equal, base angles equal, diagonals equal
    
- **Kite 鳶形**: two pairs of adjacent equal sides, one diagonal perpendicularly bisects the other
    
- **Proof tips**: use side/angle/diagonal conditions

### Chapter 7 – Areas and Volumes (III) 面積與體積 (III)

- **Pyramid 棱錐**: Volume = (1/3) × base area × height  
    Surface area = base area + lateral area (triangular faces)
    
- **Cone 圓錐**: Volume = (1/3)πr²h  
    Curved surface area = πrℓ (ℓ = slant height)  
    Total surface area = πr² + πrℓ
    
- **Sphere 球體**: Volume = (4/3)πr³  
    Surface area = 4πr²
    
- **Frustum 棱台/圓台**:  
    Volume = (1/3)h(A + B + √(AB)) for prisms; for cone frustum: (1/3)πh(R² + Rr + r²)
    
- **Similar solids 相似立體**: linear ratio k → area ratio k², volume ratio k³
    
- **Composite solids 複合立體**: split into basic solids, add/subtract volumes/areas

### Chapter 8 – Coordinate Geometry of Straight Lines 直線的坐標幾何

- **Distance formula**: d = √[(x₂−x₁)² + (y₂−y₁)²]
    
- **Midpoint formula**: ((x₁+x₂)/2 , (y₁+y₂)/2)
    
- **Slope 斜率**: m = (y₂−y₁)/(x₂−x₁) (horizontal: m=0, vertical: undefined)
    
- **Equation forms**:
    
    - Point-slope: y − y₁ = m(x − x₁)
        
    - Slope-intercept: y = mx + c
        
    - Two-point form
        
    - General form: Ax + By + C = 0
        
- **Parallel condition**: m₁ = m₂
    
- **Perpendicular condition**: m₁·m₂ = −1
    
- **Intersection point**: solve system of equations
    
- **Distance from point to line**: d = |Ax₀+By₀+C| / √(A²+B²)

### Chapter 9 – Trigonometric Relations 三角關係

- **Basic identities**:  
    sin²θ + cos²θ = 1  
    tanθ = sinθ/cosθ (cosθ≠0)
    
- **Cofunction relations (0°≤θ≤90°) 餘角關係**:  
    sin(90°−θ) = cosθ, cos(90°−θ) = sinθ, tan(90°−θ) = cotθ
    
- **Supplementary relations (θ acute) 補角關係**:  
    sin(180°−θ) = sinθ, cos(180°−θ) = −cosθ, tan(180°−θ) = −tanθ
    
- **Special angles**: memorise sin, cos, tan for 0°,30°,45°,60°,90°
    
- **Simplification & proofs**: use identities
    
- **Simple trigonometric equations**: e.g., sinθ = k, find θ in given range

### Chapter 10 – Applications of Trigonometry 三角學的應用

- **Angle of elevation 仰角** (above horizontal), **angle of depression 俯角** (below horizontal)
    
- **Bearing 方位**: Compass bearing (e.g., N30°E) or true bearing (e.g., 030°)
    
- **Right-angled triangle solving**: SOH CAH TOA
    
- **Sine rule 正弦定理** (any triangle): a/sinA = b/sinB = c/sinC = 2R
    
- **Cosine rule 餘弦定理**:  
    a² = b² + c² − 2bc cosA  
    cosA = (b² + c² − a²)/(2bc)
    
- **Area of triangle**:  
    (1/2)×base×height, (1/2)ab sinC, Heron’s formula
    
- **Applications**: height/distance measurement, navigation, architecture

### Chapter 12 – Trigonometric Ratios 三角比 (Book 2B)

- **Definitions in right triangle**:  
    sinθ = opposite/hypotenuse  
    cosθ = adjacent/hypotenuse  
    tanθ = opposite/adjacent
    
- **Calculator use**: find trig values and inverse trig (ensure DEG mode)
    
- **Exact values for special angles**:  
    sin30°=1/2, cos30°=√3/2, tan30°=1/√3  
    sin45°=cos45°=1/√2, tan45°=1  
    sin60°=√3/2, cos60°=1/2, tan60°=√3
    
- **Solving right triangles**: given two sides or one side + one angle
    
- **Slope and inclination 傾角**: line slope m = tanθ (θ≠90°, 0°≤θ<180°)
    
- **Supplementary angle relations** may extend to obtuse angles
