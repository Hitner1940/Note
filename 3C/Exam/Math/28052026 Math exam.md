## 📘 Chapter 4 – Percentages (II) 百分數 (II)

### 1. Percentage Change 百分變化

- **Formula**:  
    `Percentage change = (New value − Original value) / Original value × 100%`
    
- **New value** = Original × (1 + percentage change)  
    _Increase: + , Decrease: –_
    

### 2. Profit / Loss 盈利 / 虧蝕

- `Profit% = (Selling price − Cost) / Cost × 100%`
    
- `Loss% = (Cost − Selling price) / Cost × 100%`
    

### 3. Discount & Marked Price 折扣與標價

- `Selling price = Marked price × (1 − Discount%)`
    
- `Marked price = Cost × (1 + Markup%)`
    

### 4. Successive Percentage Changes 連續百分變化

- Overall change factor = (1 + r₁) × (1 + r₂) × …
    
- Example: Increase 10% then decrease 5% → final factor = 1.10 × 0.95 = 1.045 → overall +4.5%
    

### 5. Simple Interest 單利息

- `Interest = Principal × Rate × Time` (Time in years)
    
- `Amount = Principal + Interest = P(1 + rt)`
    

### 6. Compound Interest 複利息

- `Amount = P(1 + r)ⁿ` (r = interest rate per period, n = number of periods)
    
- `Compound interest = Amount − Principal`
    
- Compounding periods: yearly, half‑yearly, quarterly, monthly.
    

### 7. Salaries Tax (HK) 薪俸税

- `Net chargeable income = Annual income − Allowances`
    
- Tax rates (2025/26):  
    First $50,000 → 2%  
    Next $50,000 → 6%  
    Next $50,000 → 10%  
    Next $50,000 → 14%  
    Remainder → 17%
    
- If tax calculated > 15% of total income → pay 15% of total income.

## 📐 Chapter 5 – Quadrilaterals 四邊形

### 1. Properties of a Parallelogram 平行四邊形

- Opposite sides parallel and equal
    
- Opposite angles equal
    
- Adjacent angles supplementary
    
- Diagonals bisect each other
    

### 2. Conditions for a Parallelogram

- Both pairs opposite sides equal
    
- Both pairs opposite angles equal
    
- Diagonals bisect each other
    
- One pair of opposite sides both equal and parallel
    

### 3. Rhombus 菱形

- All parallelogram properties + all sides equal
    
- Diagonals perpendicular bisectors of each other
    
- Diagonals bisect interior angles
    

### 4. Rectangle 矩形

- All parallelogram properties + all angles 90°
    
- Diagonals equal
    

### 5. Square 正方形

- All rectangle properties + all rhombus properties
    

### 6. Mid‑point Theorem 中點定理

- In a triangle, segment joining mid‑points of two sides is **parallel** to the third side and **half its length**.
    

### 7. Trapezium 梯形 (Trapezoid)

- One pair of parallel sides
    
- Isosceles trapezium: legs equal, base angles equal, diagonals equal

## 🧊 Chapter 7 – Areas and Volumes (III) 面積與體積 (III)

### 1. Pyramid 棱錐

- `Volume = 1/3 × Base area × Height`
    
- Total surface area = Base area + sum of lateral (triangular) faces
    

### 2. Cone 圓錐

- `Volume = 1/3 × πr²h`
    
- `Curved surface area = πrℓ` (ℓ = slant height, ℓ² = r² + h²)
    
- `Total surface area = πr² + πrℓ`
    

### 3. Sphere 球體

- `Volume = 4/3 × πr³`
    
- `Surface area = 4πr²`
    
- Hemisphere: volume = 2/3 πr³, curved surface = 2πr², total = 3πr²
    

### 4. Frustum of a Cone/Pyramid 平截頭體

- Volume = volume of big cone/pyramid − volume of small top
    
- Or use formula: `V = 1/3 × h × (A₁ + A₂ + √(A₁A₂))` (for similar bases)
    

### 5. Similar Solids 相似立體

- Linear ratio k : 1 → Area ratio = k² : 1, Volume ratio = k³ : 1

## 📈 Chapter 8 – Coordinate Geometry of Straight Lines 直線的坐標幾何

### 1. Distance Formula

- `d = √[(x₂−x₁)² + (y₂−y₁)²]`
    

### 2. Mid‑point Formula 中點

- `M = ((x₁+x₂)/2 , (y₁+y₂)/2)`
    

### 3. Slope 斜率

- `m = (y₂−y₁) / (x₂−x₁)`
    
- Horizontal line: m = 0 ; Vertical line: m undefined
    

### 4. Equation of a Straight Line

- Point‑slope: `y − y₁ = m(x − x₁)`
    
- Slope‑intercept: `y = mx + c` (c = y‑intercept)
    
- General form: `Ax + By + C = 0`
    

### 5. Parallel & Perpendicular Lines 平行與垂直

- Parallel: `m₁ = m₂`
    
- Perpendicular: `m₁ × m₂ = −1` (except horizontal/vertical)
    

### 6. Point‑to‑Line Distance

- `d = |Ax₀ + By₀ + C| / √(A² + B²)`
    

### 7. Section Formula (Internal Division) 內分點

- Point dividing `A(x₁,y₁)` and `B(x₂,y₂)` in ratio `m:n` :  
    `((nx₁+mx₂)/(m+n) , (ny₁+my₂)/(m+n))`

## 🔁 Chapter 9 – Trigonometric Relations 三角關係

### 1. Basic Identities

- `sin²θ + cos²θ = 1`
    
- `tanθ = sinθ / cosθ`
    

### 2. Cofunction Relations (acute angles) 餘角關係

- `sin(90°−θ) = cosθ`
    
- `cos(90°−θ) = sinθ`
    
- `tan(90°−θ) = cotθ = 1/tanθ`
    

### 3. Supplementary Relations (θ acute) 補角關係

- `sin(180°−θ) = sinθ`
    
- `cos(180°−θ) = −cosθ`
    
- `tan(180°−θ) = −tanθ`
    

### 4. Special Angles Table (exact values)

|θ|sinθ|cosθ|tanθ|
|---|---|---|---|
|30°|1/2|√3/2|1/√3|
|45°|1/√2|1/√2|1|
|60°|√3/2|1/2|√3|

### 5. Finding other ratios from one (without θ)

- Draw right triangle, use Pythagoras, then definition.

## 📡 Chapter 10 – Applications of Trigonometry 三角學的應用

### 1. Gradient & Inclination 斜率與傾角

- `Gradient = tanθ = vertical rise / horizontal run`
    
- Often given as `1 : n` (e.g., 1:5 means tanθ = 1/5)
    

### 2. Angles of Elevation & Depression 仰角與俯角

- Elevation: above horizontal
    
- Depression: below horizontal
    

### 3. Bearings 方位

- **True bearing** (3‑digit angle clockwise from North, e.g., 060°)
    
- **Compass bearing** (e.g., N30°E, S45°W)
    

### 4. Sine Rule 正弦定理 (any triangle)

- `a / sin A = b / sin B = c / sin C = 2R`
    

### 5. Cosine Rule 餘弦定理

- `a² = b² + c² − 2bc·cos A`
    
- `cos A = (b² + c² − a²) / (2bc)`
    

### 6. Area of Triangle

- `½ × a × b × sin C`
    
- Heron’s formula: `√[s(s−a)(s−b)(s−c)]` with `s = (a+b+c)/2`
    

### 7. Problems with Contour Maps (scale)

- Vertical distance = difference in contour values
    
- Horizontal distance = map distance × scale
    
- Gradient = vertical / horizontal
