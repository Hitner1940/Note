## ✍️ Part 2: Grammar Focus (語法重點)

Grammar 唔好死背，要明佢嘅 logic。以下係四大必考 Grammar rules：

### 🎬 1. Defining Relative Clauses (關係子句) - _Casting Call_

用嚟補充說明 (Person, Thing, Time/Place)。

- **Person (人):**
    
    - 代替 Subject (主語): `who` / `that`
        
    - 代替 Object (賓語): `who` / `whom` / `that` (_Tip: "whom" 比較 formal，日常多數用 "who"_)
        
    - 代替 Possessive (擁有權): `whose`
        
- **Thing (死物):**
    
    - 代替 Subject / Object: `which` / `that`
        
    - 代替 Possessive: `whose`
        
- **Time / Place (時間/地點):** `when` / `where`
    

✂️ **Director's Cut: 幾時可以省略 Relative Pronouns?**

- 🔒 **LOCKED Scissors (不可省略):** 當 Pronoun 代替 Subject 或 whose。
    
    - ❌ _Error:_ The boy who ~~he~~ lives near my home is Johnny. (唔好重複 subject!)
        
    - ✅ _Pass:_ The boy **who lives** near my home is Johnny.
        
- 🔓 **UNLOCKED Scissors (可以省略):** 當 Pronoun 代替 Object。
    
    - ❌ _Error:_ This is the T-shirt that I bought ~~it~~. (唔好重複 object!)
        
    - ✅ _Pass:_ This is the T-shirt **(that) I bought**.
        

### 🧩 2. Concession (讓步句) - _The Clause/Phrase Puzzle_

想放棄用 "But"，轉用進階句型令文章升級？記熟呢個 Formula！

- **Formula A:** `Although` / `Though` + **[Clause: Subject + Verb 完整句子]**
    
    - _Test Case:_ Although **the weather was bad**, we enjoyed the concert.
        
- **Formula B:** `Despite` / `In spite of` + **[Noun Phrase / V-ing 名詞短語]**
    
    - _Test Case:_ Despite **the bad weather**, we enjoyed the concert.
        
    - 💀 **FATAL ERROR:** `Despite` 後面絕對唔可以跟完整句子！(e.g., ~~Despite the weather was bad~~ -> 必死無疑！)
        

### 🌌 3. The Multiverse of Conditionals (條件句深度圖)

- **Level 0 (General Truth 真理):** `If + Present, Present.` (100% Reality)
    
    - _Example:_ If you heat ice, it melts. (必定發生)
        
- **Level 1 (Real/Possible 有可能):** `If + Present, Future (will/can/may + V).`
    
    - _Example:_ If I study hard, I will pass the exam. (努力就有機會)
        
- **Level 2 (Unreal Present 與現在事實相反 / FF):** `If + Past, would/could/might + V.`
    
    - _Example:_ If I were a billionaire, I would buy the company. (現實我唔係億萬富翁)
        
- **Level 3 (Unreal Past 與過去事實相反 / 後悔):** `If + Past Perfect (had p.p.), would have + p.p.`
    
    - _Example:_ If I had woken up earlier, I wouldn't have missed the bus. (早知當時起身早啲啦... 現實係已經遲咗)
        

### ⏱️ 4. Tenses (時態) - _Past Simple vs. Present Perfect_

- 🎞️ **Past Simple (The Cut Shot):** 發生咗，完結咗，通常有**明確過去時間**。
    
    - _Clues:_ yesterday, in 2020, last night.
        
    - _Example:_ I **watched** the performance last night.
        
- 🎥 **Present Perfect (The Tracking Shot):** 過去發生嘅事，但**影響到現在**，或者**未完結**。
    
    - _Clues:_ already, yet, since, for, recently.
        
    - _Example:_ I **have watched** this mind-blowing performance three times already.
